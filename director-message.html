Director's Message

Educating Students For Success In A Changing World

Dear Parents,

Education is the highest function of civilization and it is the only resource that make us unique creature in this wonderful word. We believe, schools are the fertile soil that beget education and Mentors are the farmers who shower knowledge for better growth of plants who are pupils of schools.

Keeping above beliefs in our mind we have laid the foundation of City Public School, a branch of Vidyawati Foundation. It is our observation to furnish our school with sophisticated Library, Laboratory and Authentic Instruments in order to inculcate all round development in our pupils. We do believe, tentative education conjugated with computer revolution has become indispensable for students; therefore, we have been equipping our school with proficient and dexterous teachers and aides related to computer.

We want to provide a sanctified environment to our students is order to generate in them prolific qualities; determination, confidence, honesty, morality and above all sense of Universal Brotherhood So as they should be always victorious in every strife’s of life.

"Appreciation is the memory of heart" thereby, we thank parents whose moral support, firm faith, well department and admiration have emerged our institution into one of the leading institutions in Gaya and we anticipate to get above virtues of them even in coming days.

We truly believe that an optimistic suggestion can bring a remarkable and miraculous change in the betterment of our school, for, suggestions of all and sundry are welcomed.

Thank You.

S.K. Mehta
DIRECTOR, CITY PUBLIC SCHOOL
