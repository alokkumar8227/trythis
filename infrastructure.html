CAMPUS DETAILS

In congruence with the policy of healthy eating, the school does not provide 'junk food' and aerated drinks. The campus is surrounded with the harvested greenery, the pollution free zone, fresh airy area keeps the environment healthy and infection free with the help of continuous efforts of cleaning each part of the school. The vast open area on the campus provides the space for the morning assembly and other cultural activities.
City Public School has a big campus with connectivity via highway. A good system of security in the campus. It has three stairs building consisting of 40 spacious rooms with surveillance camera. It has a big library, computer lab audio-visual room, Maths Science lab. Big and different type of ground for sports. A big hall for indoor games.
